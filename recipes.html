Biryani Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or lamb
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 3 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse rice and soak for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry onions until golden and set aside.
  3. Cook meat with spices, tomatoes, and yogurt until tender.
  4. Boil water, add soaked rice and salt, cook until 70% done.
  5. Layer rice and meat mixture, sprinkle fried onions and herbs.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes (dum cooking).
  7. Fluff up the rice and serve hot.
Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or bacon
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta and garlic in olive oil until crisp.
  3. Beat eggs with cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drain pasta and quickly mix with pancetta and egg mixture off heat.
  5. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan.
Fried Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8 pieces chicken (drumsticks or thighs)
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Mix flour, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Coat the chicken pieces in the flour mixture, shaking off excess.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry chicken pieces in batches until golden brown and cooked through (about 12-15 minutes).
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
Pancit Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g bihon (rice noodles)
  • 1 cup sliced pork or chicken
  • 1 cup sliced cabbage
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 cup green beans
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Soak noodles in water for 10 minutes and drain.
  2. Sauté garlic and onion, then add meat and cook thoroughly.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  4. Pour in soy sauce, add noodles, and mix well.
  5. Cook until noodles are soft and well coated.
Adobo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken or pork
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add meat and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in soy sauce and vinegar, add bay leaves and peppercorns.
  4. Simmer until meat is tender and sauce is reduced.
  5. Adjust seasoning as needed and serve with rice.
Sinigang Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly or ribs
  • 1 packet sinigang mix (tamarind)
  • 1 tomato, quartered
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 1 cup radish slices
  • 1 cup eggplant slices
  • 1 cup string beans
  • 1 cup water spinach (kangkong)
  • 6 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Boil pork with onions and tomatoes until tender.
  2. Add sinigang mix and vegetables (except kangkong).
  3. Simmer until vegetables are cooked.
  4. Add kangkong last, simmer for 2 minutes, then serve hot.
Kare-Kare Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g oxtail or beef shank
  • 1 banana blossom (optional)
  • 1 bundle string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 banana heart, sliced (optional)
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup ground rice or rice flour
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Salt and fish sauce to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil oxtail or beef until tender, set aside broth.
  2. Sauté garlic and onion, then add meat and peanut butter.
  3. Pour in broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Add rice flour dissolved in water to thicken sauce.
  5. Add vegetables and cook until just tender.
  6. Serve hot with bagoong (shrimp paste) on the side.
Lumpia Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground pork or beef
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lumpia wrappers
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Combine all filling ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Place 1 tbsp of filling on a wrapper and roll tightly, sealing the edge with water.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry lumpia until golden brown.
  4. Drain on paper towels and serve with sweet chili sauce or vinegar.
