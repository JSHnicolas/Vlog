Biryani Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or lamb
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 3 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse rice and soak for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry onions until golden and set aside.
  3. Cook meat with spices, tomatoes, and yogurt until tender.
  4. Boil water, add soaked rice and salt, cook until 70% done.
  5. Layer rice and meat mixture, sprinkle fried onions and herbs.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes (dum cooking).
  7. Fluff up the rice and serve hot.
Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or bacon
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta and garlic in olive oil until crisp.
  3. Beat eggs with cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drain pasta and quickly mix with pancetta and egg mixture off heat.
  5. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan.
Fried Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8 pieces chicken (drumsticks or thighs)
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Mix flour, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Coat the chicken pieces in the flour mixture, shaking off excess.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry chicken pieces in batches until golden brown and cooked through (about 12-15 minutes).
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.

About Us

History

Our culinary journey began in a small family kitchen where recipes were passed down through generations, each one seasoned with tradition and love. What started as a simple hobby of documenting home-cooked meals quickly turned into a passion for sharing stories through food.

In 2020, we launched our blog to create a space where home cooks of all skill levels could come together, learn, and get inspired. As our community grew, so did our mission to showcase the diversity of global flavors, celebrate cultural heritage, and make cooking both enjoyable and accessible.

From humble beginnings to becoming a go-to resource for delicious and dependable recipes, our history is rooted in authenticity, community, and the joy of every shared meal.
